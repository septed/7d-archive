Stay focused without distractions

Firefox is designed to work with your attention, not compete for it. Reader mode clears distractions from articles, picture-in-picture keeps video out of your way, and pinned tabs keep what matters close without clutter.

Your workspace, your rules

  • Tab groups and Firefox View help you manage the chaos
  • Customizable sidebar keeps your most-used tools one click away
  • Clean interface and intuitive controls keep clutter out of your way

AI, if and when you want it

Firefox approaches AI with intention, not obligation. AI features are optional by design and will remain that way.

Firefox protects your data

Firefox is built around clear privacy principles. We’re upfront about what data we collect, what we don’t, and how we use it.
