Thoughts on Mystic Messenger after all (Spoilerfree)

 [!!! WARNING THE NON-SPOILER IS ONLY FOR NEW CONTENT: Ray’s After Ending & Jumin’s BE2]

Spoilers in this:

- V’s After Ending

- Another Story

- Original Story & SEs

So yesterday, the new content finally dropped, and what a trip it was getting to here. I am positive that  @cheritzteam worked so hard for the After Ending to come out over the years, and it does show. The amount of CGs and content is astounding. Some of the CGs are mesmerizingly beautiful, and I will be doing edits with them in the future. I am already working on some that I will let drop after the new year. But this is where my praise ends for now. I will return to this when I have finished EVERYTHING (All BEs etc.), and Ray’s After Ending has spread more amongst the remaining fandom.

So here are my thoughts. Less is more. A doctrine that Cheritz encapsulated so beautifully in their early days. It is no secret that I am primarily an OS fan. AS never spoke to me much, but I accepted it as canon. Even if the timelines got messed up and I preferred the art created under the direction of one Mint Bunny (Minju Chung). 

I never really wanted to romance either V or Saeran, but I got why some people might. I do not remember much of how I felt from playing V’s route. Mostly it was just finding new information and broadening my knowledge about the established universe, finding out what happened. Ray’s route made me feel like I was Belle in Beauty and the Beast, only reversed. This time the prince appearing at the end of the movie was breathing down my neck with his candy floss breath and sticking to me like gum at every turn, and I felt claustrophobic and freaked out more than anything else. There was nothing healthy or redeeming about what unfolded on screen. Ray was the creature and Rika Dr. Frankenstein. He managed to give me a couple of good scares, and I guess, in that sense, it was thrilling?

Still, I was happy to save both men. Most likely V more than Saeran, as he went away at the end of his route, and I got a 2-year break away from him. And when V’s AE dropped last year together with Rika’s Background Story. I was genuinely pleased, unlike most of the fandom. Here came a story that ended with everyone in the RFA (even Rika) being happy at the end (Forgive, not judge), and maybe putting MC together with V was the best solution. I have never really cared about self-inserting myself into the game. To me, MC was as much a character in the game as the rest. I just got to be her puppet master and indirectly influence the rest of them.

I get why both V fans and others might not like either content. After all, where was V during this whole storyline? Out traveling being a “good” guy. That is the V we know. But still unsatisfactory to the romance crowd. I was happy to see Rika find some kind of freedom. I never wished for any of the MM characters to suffer. And the SEs left us hanging with secrecy, murder, and doubt hanging between the remaining group members like the disease of secrecy had spread out between them now V and Rika were incapacitated. Not a happy ending after all.

So what could I expect from this seemingly last hurrah of these last 4 ½ years since launch? Certainly not what I got. I have seen plenty of people praise this DLC for being wholesome and the best of what they hoped for. I wish I could be one of those people myself. But I can’t.

Because I can not look away when I see the major logical holes in the plot, the writing that made every other character seem like nothing more than the function they had in this after end as it abandons every previously mentioned fact in its wake. The warped mentality forces the puppet master (you) to choose a cruel and selfish route into all-consuming and blind love that disregards anything and anyone else. And in the end, the resolution that this should be the right choice? No, these can not be the right choices. 

So many people ended up getting Bad End on day four because those choices weren’t right. And now I wonder if Cheritz truly sees this fandom’s MCs as nothing more than cruel and demanding monsters that will rest at nothing to get their happy ending no matter if it costs their integrity.

// LOLKAT
